# User Interview — Job Your Love

- **Who:** A job seeker who tracks applications across several companies
- **When:** 2026-07-09
- **How:** Live-app walkthrough and interview, about 15 minutes

## What they do today (without your project)

They currently track jobs with a spreadsheet and separate notes. The hard part is remembering which applications need follow-up and quickly seeing which opportunities are active, rejected, or moving to interviews.

## What they liked

- The dashboard stats are easy to understand at a glance.
- The status labels make the application pipeline clear.
- The table layout feels familiar because it works like a spreadsheet.

## What confused them / what's missing

- Follow-up dates and next steps are not visible enough from the main dashboard.
- They expected time-sensitive tasks to appear without opening each application detail page.

## What would make them actually use it

- A stronger follow-up/reminder view for upcoming tasks.
- A dashboard section that surfaces the next action for active applications.

## What I'll change (next steps)

- [ ] Add a dashboard section for upcoming follow-ups.
- [ ] Add clearer next-step visibility for each active application.
